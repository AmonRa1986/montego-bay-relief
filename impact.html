Stories from Hurricane Melissa

These are real stories from families affected by Hurricane Melissa in Montego Bay. These are the people we're working to help.

Westgreen: "We Lost Everything"

"The water came so fast. We grabbed the children and ran to the roof. When we came down, everything was gone. Our clothes, our food, the children's school books—all underwater. We're sleeping on wet mattresses with no blankets. The children are cold at night."

— Maria, mother of three in Westgreen

Black River: Farmer's Livelihood Destroyed

"I've been farming this land for 30 years. Hurricane Melissa destroyed my entire crop—everything I planted for the season. My tools are ruined. I don't know how I'll feed my family or plant again. This was our only income."

— Winston, farmer in Black River

Montego Bay: Children Can't Return to School

"My daughter's school uniform was destroyed. Her books are ruined. She's supposed to start classes next week, but I have no money to replace anything. She's crying because she wants to go back to school, but I can't send her like this."

— Patricia, single mother in Montego Bay

How We Measure Impact

Distribution Tracking

Every relief kit is logged with recipient name, location, and date. Local coordinators verify delivery and collect feedback from families.

Photo Documentation

We document every distribution with photos (with family permission) to show donors exactly where their contributions go and who they help.

Financial Reporting

Quarterly financial reports show exactly how donations were spent, with receipts for all purchases and transparent accounting.

Beneficiary Feedback

We collect feedback from families receiving aid to ensure our programs meet their actual needs and maintain dignity in delivery.

Dollar-for-Dollar Tracking

Every dollar donated is tracked from receipt to final distribution, with public reporting on how funds are allocated across programs.

Progress Updates

Monthly email updates to donors with current progress toward goals, upcoming distributions, and stories from the field.

Our Transparency Commitment

As a newly incorporated nonprofit, we're building trust through radical transparency. We believe donors deserve to know exactly how their money is used and what impact it creates.

What We Promise:

  • ✓ 100% of donations go directly to program funding (relief kits, education, rebuilding)
  • ✓ Quarterly financial reports with receipts and expense breakdowns
  • ✓ Photo documentation of every distribution (with family consent)
  • ✓ Monthly progress updates toward our goals
  • ✓ Public reporting on our website and social media
  • ✓ Donor communication - we respond to every question about how funds are used
