Our Hurricane Relief Programs

Three core programs designed to provide immediate relief and long-term recovery support for families affected by Hurricane Melissa in Montego Bay, Jamaica.

Emergency Relief Kits

Immediate disaster relief for families who lost everything to Hurricane Melissa. Each kit provides essential supplies to help families survive and begin recovery.

What's Included:

  • • Food staples: Rice, beans, canned goods, cooking oil (2-week supply for family of 4)
  • • Water purification: Filtration tablets and storage containers
  • • Hygiene supplies: Soap, toothpaste, sanitary products, diapers
  • • First aid: Bandages, antiseptic, pain relievers, prescription support
  • • Emergency shelter: Tarps, rope, flashlights, batteries

Cost per kit: $25

Your $25 donation provides one complete relief kit for a family in need.

Education Support

Ensuring children can continue their education after the hurricane. We provide school supplies, uniforms, and learning resources so students don't fall behind.

What We Provide:

  • • School supplies: Notebooks, pens, pencils, backpacks, calculators
  • • Uniforms: Required school uniforms and shoes for students
  • • Books & materials: Textbooks and learning materials damaged in the hurricane
  • • Technology access: Tablets and internet access for remote learning

Goal: Support 300 students

Help us ensure every child can return to school and continue their education.

Community Rebuilding

Long-term rebuilding support to help families restore their homes, businesses, and livelihoods. We provide materials, tools, and skilled labor coordination.

Our Rebuilding Efforts:

  • • Home repairs: Roofing materials, lumber, tools for structural repairs
  • • Business recovery: Equipment and inventory replacement for small businesses
  • • Infrastructure: Community center repairs, road clearing, water system restoration
  • • Livelihood support: Agricultural tools and seeds for farmers who lost crops

Long-term commitment

Rebuilding takes time. We're committed to supporting families through every step of recovery.

How We Deliver Programs

Community-Led Distribution

We work directly with local leaders in Westgreen, Black River, and Montego Bay who know which families need help most. Relief is delivered with dignity and cultural understanding.

Transparent Tracking

Every relief kit, every school supply, every building material is documented. Donors receive regular updates with photos, receipts, and impact reports.

100% Direct Funding

All donations go directly to program costs. Administrative expenses are covered separately to ensure maximum impact from your contribution.
