Our Mission: Jamaica Disaster Relief & Community Development

Montego Bay Relief, Inc. is a U.S.-incorporated nonprofit organization (501c3 pending) dedicated to providing emergency disaster relief and sustainable community development in Montego Bay, Jamaica, and the surrounding St. James Parish region.

Founded in response to the devastating impact of Hurricane Melissa, our mission is to support Jamaican families through immediate humanitarian aid and long-term rebuilding initiatives. We focus on three core programs: Emergency Relief Kits (50% of resources), Education Support (25%), and Community Rebuilding (25%).

Hurricane Relief & Community Development Programs

Hurricane Relief Kits & Emergency Aid

Immediate disaster relief providing food, water, hygiene supplies, and shelter materials to families who lost everything to Hurricane Melissa. 50% of all resources dedicated to emergency response.

Learn More

School Supplies & Educational Support

Ensuring children can continue their education after the hurricane by providing school supplies, uniforms, and learning resources. Supporting 300 students across Montego Bay schools.

Learn More

Community Rebuilding & Home Repairs

Long-term rebuilding support including home repairs, business recovery, and infrastructure restoration. Helping families rebuild their lives and livelihoods with dignity.
